About Me

Hello, my name is Brandon Collins. My journey into the world of web development began with a desire to avoid coding altogether. I started by exploring HTML and CSS, leveraging content management systems to build websites without delving deep into the complexities of programming. However, as I delved deeper into the field, I discovered the transformative power of JavaScript and the rapid advancements in development tools over the years.

Impressed by the potential of these technologies, I decided to dive headfirst into the world of coding and development. My goal is to become a full-stack software engineer, equipped to tackle challenges across the entire stack, from front-end interfaces to back-end logic and database management.

I am particularly drawn to the aspects of web development that involve storing and analyzing data. The ability to create dynamic, interactive experiences that respond to user input and deliver personalized content is incredibly rewarding. Additionally, I find the realm of 3D rendering fascinating, intrigued by the possibilities it offers for creating immersive digital environments.

As I continue my journey towards becoming a full-stack software engineer, I remain committed to expanding my skills and knowledge in these areas. I am excited about the opportunities ahead and look forward to contributing to innovative projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in web development.
